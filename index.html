Welcome to the Potter's Shop

The Bible says, "we are not of this world.." However, we are in the world and have a mandate to influence our world.
How then do we manage this influence and still remain untainted with the world we interact with? We seek to find answers at the Potter's Shop.

The Potter's Shop is an informal virtual meeting space where Youths meet to discuss topical and life issues as it affects our faith, walk with the Holy Spirit and our influence and interactions in the world.

The Potter's Shop is an avenue for fellowship and gisting about practical life issues and we reference scriptural concepts and precepts for solution.

The Motivation

The Christian walk is not meant to be done alone. We need one another. "Iron sharpens iron.." remember?
The focus of the interactions is for us to learn and grow. Applied learning is what initiates growth.
And the growth we seek through the Potter's Shop is not just spiritual. It's a growth that cuts across all facets of life - physical, marital, financial, career etc.
